Barbecue Wings

Description

Sweet or savory, mild or spicy, or even honey garlic wings; they’re the ultimate snack food! Thes are prepped in mere minutes and they come out hot and juicy with spicy sticky bbq sauce!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken wings
  • 1 ½ tablespoons flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • salt & pepper

Sauce

  • ⅔ cup barbecue sauce
  • ½ tablespoon sriracha
  • 2 cloves garlic

Steps

  1. Pat wings dry with paper towels & toss with dry ingredients.
  2. Bake wings for 15 minutes, flip, & bake another 15 minutes.
  3. Remove wings from oven and toss with sauce. Increase heat and bake until sticky.
